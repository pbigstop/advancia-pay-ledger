Recommendation for Seed Round

Use Concept 01 as your primary brand for pitch materials and the platform. It commands enterprise credibility while nodding to your crypto infrastructure.

Recommendation for Facility Sales

Use Concept 02 for your facility-facing materials — onboarding docs, compliance sheets, and any patient-touching interfaces.

Recommendation for Fundraising Events

Deploy Concept 03 for VC pitch nights, Product Hunt, TechCrunch announcements, and your LinkedIn founder presence.

Strategic Packaging Recommendation

Advancia doesn't need to pick one — deploy all three as a contextual brand system. Concept 01 anchors your identity (platform + investors). Concept 02 handles facility trust-building. Concept 03 fires up fundraising events and launch moments. Together they cover every audience you're targeting: enterprise buyers, healthcare operators, and seed-stage investors.
